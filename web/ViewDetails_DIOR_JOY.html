JOY by DIOR

€125

JOY by DIOR is a luminous fragrance, featuring a vibrant bouquet of flowers tinged with citrus and musk. An ode to pleasure and life.

Creator

Conceived by François Demachy, DIOR's in-house perfumer, this fragrance embodies the modern vision of DIOR's femininity.

Creation Process

Combining luxurious notes of rose and jasmine with zesty bergamot and mandarin, JOY is crafted to evoke a sense of radiant delight.

Origin

Designed and produced in France, JOY by DIOR is manufactured using the finest ingredients sourced from exclusive DIOR gardens.

Brand History

Christian Dior has been an iconic brand since 1947, known for its elegance, innovation, and the ability to bring joy and luxury to everyday life.

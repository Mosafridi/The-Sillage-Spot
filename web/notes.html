Types of Perfume Notes

Citrus Notes

Fresh and zesty, often including lemon, bergamot, and orange.

Floral Notes

Sweet and romantic, with scents like rose, jasmine, and lavender.

Woody Notes

Earthy and warm, featuring sandalwood, cedar, and patchouli.

Oriental Notes

Rich and spicy, often containing vanilla, amber, and cinnamon.

Fruity Notes

Sweet and juicy, including apple, peach, and berries.

Aquatic Notes

Fresh and oceanic, giving a cool and breezy feel.

Green Notes

Herbal and leafy, reminiscent of fresh-cut grass or tea leaves.

Gourmand Notes

Edible and dessert-like, with vanilla, chocolate, and caramel.

Leather Notes

Rich and smoky, resembling the scent of suede or tobacco.

About Versace Dylan Purple

Versace Dylan Purple Eau de Parfum is a radiant and captivating fragrance that embodies sophistication and femininity. Designed for those who exude confidence and charm, this scent is a tribute to elegance and individuality.

Fragrance Notes

  • Top Notes: Bergamot, Pear, Bitter Orange
  • Heart Notes: Freesia, Pomarose, Mahonial
  • Base Notes: Iso E Super, Cedarwood, Belambra Tree

Inspiration

Inspired by the bold and vibrant energy of the Mediterranean, Versace Dylan Purple is a scent that combines fresh, floral, and woody elements, evoking a sense of timeless glamour.

Why Choose Versace Dylan Purple?

This Eau de Parfum offers a sophisticated and long-lasting fragrance, making it perfect for both daytime wear and evening occasions. With its unique blend of fruity and floral notes, it leaves an enchanting and unforgettable trail.

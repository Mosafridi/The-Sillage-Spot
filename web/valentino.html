About Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Intense

Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Intense Eau de Parfum is a luxurious and charismatic fragrance that embodies the spirit of modern masculinity. Designed for those who embrace their individuality with confidence and sophistication, this scent is an ode to self-expression.

Fragrance Notes

  • Top Notes: Vanilla, Lavender
  • Heart Notes: Vetiver
  • Base Notes: Spicy and Woody Accords

Inspiration

Born from the vibrant streets of Rome, this fragrance blends timeless elegance with contemporary boldness. It is crafted for the modern man who carries his heritage with pride while embracing the energy of today’s world.

Why Choose Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Intense?

This Eau de Parfum offers a long-lasting and captivating scent, perfect for both casual wear and special occasions. With its rich and warm aroma, it leaves an unforgettable impression wherever you go.

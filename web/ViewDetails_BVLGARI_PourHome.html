An embodiment of elegance and masculinity, BVLGARI Pour Homme offers a discreet yet noticeable scent that combines fresh notes of Darjeeling tea with the earthiness of papyrus wood.

Creator

Crafted by renowned perfumer Jacques Cavallier, this fragrance is designed for men who appreciate the luxury of simplicity.

Creation Process

A mix of classic ingredients and modern accords, this cologne reflects a journey through the best raw materials known in perfumery.

Origin

Assembled in Italy, with a commitment to using only the highest quality components sourced from around the world.

Brand History

Since 1884, BVLGARI has been synonymous with innovation and excellence in jewelry and luxury goods, expanding its expertise into perfumes in the 1990s.

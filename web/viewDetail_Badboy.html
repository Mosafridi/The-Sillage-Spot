About Bad Boy

Bad Boy by Carolina Herrera is a bold and sophisticated fragrance that redefines masculinity with its striking contrast of elements. Launched as part of the New York Collection, this scent is designed for the modern man who embraces his duality—strong yet sensitive, powerful yet empathetic.

Fragrance Notes

  • Top Notes: Black Pepper, White Pepper, Bergamot
  • Heart Notes: Cedarwood, Sage
  • Base Notes: Tonka Bean, Cocoa

Inspiration

Inspired by the vibrant energy of New York, Bad Boy embodies the essence of rebellion and elegance. Its lightning bolt-shaped bottle symbolizes the electrifying presence of those who dare to stand out.

Why Choose Bad Boy?

Perfect for evening wear or special occasions, Bad Boy offers a long-lasting scent with a unique blend of spicy and sweet undertones, making it a must-have for those who want to leave a lasting impression.
